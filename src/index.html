About NashCocktail

Established in July, 2010. Nashcocktail is a fun evening of casual networking and cold drinks. It is a free event to attend and open to anyone interested in social media and networking.

Each month IT professionals, social media enthusiasts, marketers, developers, graphic designers, bloggers, podcasters, and the like, have gathered to meet.

Guests who RSVP are eligible to win great door prizes from our giving sponsors. Prizes have included: Bluetooth speakers, iPods, Rokus, Chromecasts, Grimey's and Amazon gift certificates, bacon (really, bacon), and much more.

Meet new people. Maybe find a new job, employee, co-founder, friend... anything is possible at Nashcocktail.

Our mixer is usually held on the fourth Tuesday of the month from 6 - 8 pm in Nashville.
